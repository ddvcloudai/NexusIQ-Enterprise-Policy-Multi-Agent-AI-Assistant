HUMAN RESOURCES DEPARTMENT POLICIES
=====================================

LEAVE AND ATTENDANCE
1. Employees must submit leave requests at least 7 calendar days in advance through the HR portal.
2. Sick leave requires a medical certificate for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
3. Annual leave entitlement is 20 days per year for employees with less than 5 years of service and 25 days for those with 5 or more years.
4. Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days into the next calendar year.
5. Public holidays falling on weekends are compensated with an alternate day off.

REMOTE WORK AND FLEXIBLE HOURS
6. Work from home (WFH) is permitted up to 2 days per week upon manager approval.
7. Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM; employees may flex the remaining hours within 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
8. Employees working remotely must remain reachable via approved communication tools during core hours.
9. Remote work privileges may be revoked for repeated performance or communication issues.

PERFORMANCE AND APPRAISAL
10. Annual performance reviews are conducted twice a year: July and December.
11. New hires are subject to a 90-day probationary review before confirmation of employment.
12. Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs) are issued when an employee receives a "Below Expectations" rating for two consecutive reviews.
13. Promotions are merit-based and require a minimum of 12 months in the current role.

ONBOARDING AND OFFBOARDING
14. All new hires must complete mandatory onboarding training within the first 2 weeks of joining.
15. Employees must serve a notice period of 30 days (junior) or 60 days (senior/managerial) upon resignation.
16. Exit interviews are mandatory and must be completed before the final working day.
17. All company assets including laptops, access cards, and documents must be returned on the last working day.

CONDUCT AND ETHICS
18. Any form of workplace harassment, discrimination, or bullying will result in immediate disciplinary action.
19. Employees must disclose conflicts of interest to their manager and HR in writing.
20. Confidential company information must not be shared externally without written authorization.
21. External event attendance requires approval through the following hierarchy: Manager → Director → HR.
22. Violation of the Code of Conduct may lead to warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity.

COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
23. Salaries are reviewed annually and adjustments are communicated during the December appraisal cycle.
24. Health insurance coverage extends to employee, spouse, and up to two dependent children.
25. Employees are eligible for a performance bonus of up to 15% of annual salary based on review outcomes.
26. Travel reimbursements must be submitted within 30 days of the travel date with original receipts.
