INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY DEPARTMENT POLICIES
==========================================

DEVICE AND HARDWARE MANAGEMENT
1. All employees are issued company devices (laptop/desktop) upon joining; personal devices must not be used for official work without IT approval.
2. Company devices must not be used for personal activities, gaming, cryptocurrency mining, or accessing non-work-related streaming platforms.
3. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to the IT helpdesk within 2 hours of discovery.
4. Hardware upgrades or replacements must be requested through the IT portal and are subject to approval and budget availability.
5. Devices must not be physically modified, and unauthorized peripherals (e.g., external GPUs) must not be connected.

SOFTWARE AND LICENSING
6. Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices; installation of unauthorized software is a policy violation.
7. All software licenses are managed centrally by IT; employees must not purchase or subscribe to software tools without an approved purchase request.
8. Open-source tools may be used only after a security review and written approval from the IT Security team.
9. Software subscriptions must be reviewed annually and renewed or terminated based on active usage.

CYBERSECURITY AND DATA PROTECTION
10. All employees must complete mandatory cybersecurity awareness training within 30 days of joining and annually thereafter.
11. Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters, and must not be reused within the last 10 cycles.
12. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all company systems, applications, and email.
13. Employees must not click on suspicious links or attachments; phishing attempts must be reported to security@company.com immediately.
14. Company data must not be stored on personal cloud storage (Google Drive personal, Dropbox personal, etc.).
15. Sensitive data must be encrypted when shared externally using IT-approved encryption tools.

NETWORK AND ACCESS CONTROL
16. Employees must not connect to public Wi-Fi for official work without using the company-approved VPN.
17. Access to internal systems is role-based; employees must not share credentials or attempt to access systems beyond their authorization level.
18. VPN access is provisioned by IT upon manager request and reviewed every 6 months.
19. Visitors and contractors must be granted only temporary, limited network access through the guest network.
20. All remote access sessions are logged and monitored for compliance purposes.

IT SUPPORT AND SERVICE REQUESTS
21. IT support requests must be submitted through the official helpdesk portal (helpdesk.company.com) or by emailing it-support@company.com.
22. Critical issues (e.g., system outage, security breach) must be reported by phone to the IT emergency line for priority response.
23. Standard service requests are addressed within 2 business days; critical issues within 4 hours.
24. Employees must not attempt to self-repair company hardware; all repairs are handled exclusively by authorized IT personnel.

DATA BACKUP AND RECOVERY
25. Employees must save all critical work to company-approved cloud storage (e.g., SharePoint, OneDrive for Business) to ensure automatic backup.
26. IT conducts system-wide backups daily; individual file recovery requests must be submitted within 14 days of data loss.
27. Business continuity and disaster recovery drills are conducted bi-annually; employee participation is mandatory.
